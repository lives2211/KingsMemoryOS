## Tweet 5: Clean IP Importance

Discovered why my accounts kept getting weird issues.

**The problem:**
- Account banned (not my fault)
- No algorithm boost
- Payment failures

**The culprit:**
Shared VPN nodes = "10,000 users" on same IP
Mixed with spam/abuse traffic
Already flagged by platforms

**The test:**
Switched to personal VPS with clean IP
Result: All problems gone

**The lesson:**
Your "clean" account inherits dirty IP reputation

**The solution:**
- Personal VPS (not shared)
- Residential IP services
- Avoid free VPNs for important accounts

**The cost:**
$5/month vs $0/month
Worth it for stability

Can't believe I didn't know this earlier.
